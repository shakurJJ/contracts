# ADR-003: Hash-based privacy for sensitive fields

## Context

Healthcare data includes sensitive fields that cannot be stored in plaintext on-chain.

Direct storage of patient identifiers or record contents would expose privacy and violate regulatory expectations.

## Decision

Use hash-based privacy for sensitive fields and store encrypted references rather than raw secrets.

## Consequences

- Sensitive content is kept off-chain while on-chain metadata remains auditable.
- Hashing improves integrity checks and prevents disclosure of confidential values.
- Requires careful key management for off-chain encryption and retrieval of referenced payloads.
