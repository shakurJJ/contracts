# ADR-004: Multi-sig governance threshold design

## Context

Governance actions in the healthcare suite must prevent single-party control over critical upgrades or administrative changes.

A simple owner-based model is insufficient for a distributed consortium of providers and auditors.

## Decision

Adopt a threshold multi-signature governance model where actions require multiple authorized signers.

## Consequences

- Reduces the risk of unilateral malicious upgrades.
- Enables broader participation for provider, regulator, and auditor stakeholders.
- Adds complexity to transaction submission and authorization tracking.
